-- 2, nodes that contain other nodes

I want to be able to have this message in the script,

    i assert_build node_h design_h

How to do this? One way would be to have nodes that contain other nodes. But I
am not fond of this. I would rather have ports.

I suppose you could have this,

    port our_gearbox.in
    node our_gearbox

    port our_vehicle.has our_gearbox none
    node our_vehicle

Basically it is defining a bag interface at one end, and then a baggable
interface at the other side. It is janky.

Don't do this. Instead, have assert_build work recursively.
